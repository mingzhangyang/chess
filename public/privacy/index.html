Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Cloud Chess Room is designed for fast room-based chess matches. This policy explains what data is processed when you use the service and how that data is handled.

1. Data We Process

  • Nickname and room ID you provide to join or create a room.
  • Real-time game events (moves, turn state, and room synchronization messages).
  • Optional chat, voice, and camera streams you share with other players in the room.
  • Client preferences stored locally, such as language and sound settings.

2. How We Use Data

  • Operate multiplayer rooms and maintain game state.
  • Provide real-time communication features and reconnect reliability.
  • Protect service stability and prevent abuse.

3. Storage and Retention

Room state may be stored temporarily to support active matches and reconnection. Client preferences are saved in your browser local storage. We do not require account registration for normal gameplay.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on infrastructure providers for hosting, networking, and caching. Voice/video transport may use browser WebRTC components and configured relay infrastructure.

5. Your Choices

  • You can use text-only gameplay without enabling microphone or camera access.
  • You can clear browser storage to remove local preferences.
  • You can stop using the service at any time.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the project owner through the repository or deployment contact channel.
